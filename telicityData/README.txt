interCorp_silverStandard_standoff: standoff annotations for the projected silver standard.
columns in csv file: English sentence ID, Czech sentence ID, English verb (as it occurs in the sentence), English lemma, Czech verb, Czech lemma, perfectiveness (of Czech verb) from Vallex.

In our experiments, impf (imperfective) was mapped to atelic and pf (perfective) was mapped to telic.
